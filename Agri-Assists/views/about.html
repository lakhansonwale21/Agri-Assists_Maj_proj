Our Mission

Smart Farming for Every Farmer

Agri-Assists is a comprehensive digital agriculture platform designed to empower farmers, shopkeepers, and agricultural communities across India. We provide real-time data, market intelligence, and direct connections to help every stakeholder in the agricultural ecosystem thrive.

Platform Features

What We Offer

Every tool a farmer needs, unified in one intelligent platform.

Real-Time Weather

Get accurate, location-based weather forecasts including temperature, humidity, rainfall, and wind speed to plan sowing and harvesting perfectly.

Live Market Prices

Access daily Mandi prices across states, districts, and commodities. Know the real value of your crops before you sell — filtered by your exact location.

Nearby Agro-Shops

Find verified local agricultural shops selling seeds, fertilizers, pesticides, and farming equipment. Browse their products and connect directly.

Financial Tracking

Track your income, expenses, and profits season by season. Visualize your financial data to make better business decisions for your farm.

Community Help

Got crop disease or pest problems? Raise requests to agricultural experts and community members for fast, actionable solutions.

Multilingual Support

Available in English, Hindi, and Marathi — ensuring no language barriers so every farmer can use the platform comfortably.
